Overview

Purpose

This site will be an interactive map that will list lesser known travel spots. These spots will appear within a center radius of the search area.

Audience

Indiviudals who are interested in travel and exploration, but who do not want to go to highly touristed locations.

Content

Home page

THe Home Page will be the central interacting portion of the HiddenGems website. It will display a large map with interactive points. Clicking on these points will bring up cards that will display a lesser known travel location/point of interest.

Images for the Home page

Contribute

This page will be a simple form page where individuals can input locations to add to the map.

Images for the Contribute

About

This page will display a message describing why the website was created and where it hopes to go.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The background image of the webpage will be an interactive map. On each of the continents, there will be interactive points. These points, when clicked, will bring up cards that will show an image of a point of interest, the name and location of the place, and a short summary about the point of interest.

Contribute

THe page will contain a form that would allow the viewer to input the name of a place, which continent it is on, a reason why it should be added, and their name(optional) for creditation.

About

This page will display the mission message, the reason for the site, and where I hope the site to eventually go from where it begins.
